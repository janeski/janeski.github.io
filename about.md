## Miroslav Janeski

**EngD | Technical Director | Microsoft MVP**

I'm a software engineer and technical leader with a doctorate in engineering (EngD) and over 15 years of experience building distributed systems and cloud-native applications on Azure.

I'm a **Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP)** in two categories:
- **Azure — Application as a Service (PaaS)**
- **.NET**

My technical focus areas include Azure Functions, .NET Aspire, Dapr, Azure Container Apps, IoT, and cloud-native architecture. I'm particularly interested in how modern tooling — Aspire, azd, Dapr — is changing the way we build and operate distributed systems.

I currently serve as **Technical Director** and work at the intersection of software architecture, platform engineering, and team leadership.
